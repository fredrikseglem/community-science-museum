Welcome to a World of Wonder

From the stars above to the world under a microscope, science is a grand adventure.

Over the course of human history, science has developed from our early understanding of fire, wind, water, and earth to exploring everything from galaxies far away to the very building blocks of life itself.

The aim of our museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a lifelong passion to continue the exploration of the world around us.

We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to life through over 1000 interactive exhibits. Come play, interact, and see science in action (but watch out for the dinosaurs at night!).

Our Exhibition Spaces

Dive into the fascinating worlds of science and technology.

Cosmology

Explore the wonders of our cosmos in ‘The Sky Above Us’. Learn about constellations, galaxies, and the history of solar exploration from Galileo to today.

Evolution

Discover the origins of life on this planet and meet creatures who didn't survive, including our Woolly Mammoth, Dodos, and dinosaurs.

Biology and Medicine

From micro-organisms to the human body, explore the history of medicine, penicillin, and the first heart transplant.

Robotics and AI

The information revolution is here. Explore the science of the future, from home AI to industrial robotics. You can even shake hands with Rob the Robot!

Ecology

Learn about the impact humans have on the planet, the dangers of global warming, and the positive ways we can all contribute to a brighter, sustainable future.

Interactive Physics

Our exhibits are designed to be hands-on. See the principles of physics come to life as you interact with displays that demonstrate energy, motion, and more.
